Water Cycle Processes

🌊 Evaporation

Heat from the sun causes water from oceans, lakes, and rivers to change from liquid to gas (water vapor)

🌳 Transpiration

Plants absorb water through roots and release water vapor through their leaves

☁️ Condensation

Water vapor cools and changes back to liquid droplets, forming clouds and fog

🌧️ Precipitation

Water falls from clouds as rain, snow, sleet, or hail

🏔️ Runoff & Infiltration

Water flows over land surface or soaks into the ground to become groundwater
